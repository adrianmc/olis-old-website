HOW OLIS HELPS

Here are some of the ways Olis helps to keep your company productive.

Making Chat Actionable and Smart

No one has time to read all chat messages. Sometimes you just want someone to tell you the actionable items from a long chat conversation.

With Olis, you can create actionable task items and summaries from individual chat messages. This way, if you want to know the context behind how a task was created, you can easily check the history that is linked to the task. A smart analytical algorithm figures out the relevant context for each task.

Each task can then be managed in a kanban-board style for maximal productivity and simplicity.

Powerful and Convenient Translation

Not only will Olis be launched with multiple Asian languages in its user interface, Olis also features powerful translation functionality.

Enterprises in Asia often have to deal with ideas and people from a variety of countries. We are able to offer simple one-click translations of individual chat messages.

Over 50 languages is supported so you'll never be left wondering what something means.

Multi-faceted Collaboration

With Olis, you have the ability to collaboratively work on documents, spreadsheets, and comment on pictures.

Often, you may even want to share your screen so that you can show your teammates exactly what you are working on.

With clients and vendors that may not use Olis, you can grant them secure guest access in order to give them a voice in your team's conversation.

Harnessing Ideas

Give your company the ability to run polls and brainstorming sessions that thousands can participate in. What's more, these sessions can be anonymized to facilitate more active and creative collaboration in brainstorming.

Individual chat messages can also be voted upon so that teammates can provide feedback as a chat is on-going.

Work without Distractions

Another way to reduce phone calls, emails, and other interruptions of the day. Manage the path to your (virtual) door by taking control of how information reaches you.

In order to keep you productive, we have implemented fully customizable alert settings that allow you to be notified only when your name is mentioned, or if someone directly messages you.
